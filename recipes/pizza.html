Fast Pizza

Description

A fast and tasty pizza you can eat every day.

Ingredients

  • 450g wheat flour
  • 4g dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100ml warm water
  • 150g yoghurt
  • 50 ml oil
  • Tomatello
  • Whatever you want to put on the pizza
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine flour, dry yeast, sugar and salt in a bowl together.
  2. Combine warm water, yoghurt and oil in a bowl together.
  3. Put half of the flour in the liquid and mix it all together.
  4. Add half of the leftover flour again, and then knead it for a bit, and then combine and knead everything.
  5. Let it rest for 45 minutes.
  6. Knead it again and then cover it for 10 minutes. Meanwhile warm the oven to max temperature
  7. Spread the dough and put whatever you want on it.
  8. Put the pizza in the oven and let it bake for around 10 minutes.
