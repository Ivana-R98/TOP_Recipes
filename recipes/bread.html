Homemade Bread

Description

One of many homemade bread recipes that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 500ml warm water
  • 1 tbsp dry yeast (10-15g)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 700g wheat flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Combine warm water, dry yeast and sugar and leave it for 5 minutes;
  2. Combine 600g flour and salt;
  3. Mix the previous two steps together;
  4. Add two tbsp of oil and 100g flour to the dough and knead;
  5. Add two more tablespoons of oil and a bit more flour and knead again;
  6. Leave it for an hour;
  7. Knead a bit more and then let it rest for another 10 minutes;
  8. Shape it however you would like and set it up in the baking tray;
  9. While you let it rest for 10 minutes, preheat the oven to 220 degrees;
  10. You can coat it with water and sprinkle it with flour or palenta;
  11. Bake it in the oven at 220 degrees for 30 minutes.
