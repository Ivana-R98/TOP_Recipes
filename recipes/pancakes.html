Pancakes

Description

Sweet or salty, full or empty, simple yet tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g wheat flour
  • 250ml milk
  • 200ml mineral water
  • 1 tbsp vanilla sugar
  • a pinch of salt
  • a little bit of oil

Steps

  1. Mix eggs well, then add a bit of salt and vanilla sugar;
  2. Add half of the milk and flour and mix;
  3. Combine the leftover milk and flour and mix again;
  4. Add a bit of oil, the mineral water;
  5. Mix well so that there are no lumps left;
  6. Leave the mixture for at least half an hour in the fridge;
  7. Fry them in a oiled pan and then put whatever you'd like on them (our favourites are euro cream, biscuits and bananas);
  8. Enjoy as much as you would like!
